Claire Nelson

The Thoughtful Dietitian

I am an Accredited Practicing Dietitian (APD). My PhD research has focused on nutrition status of community-dwelling adults with chronic lower leg wounds. In my academic roles I have worked with both nutrition and non-nutrition students to help them gain a greater understanding of the role of nutrition in health. I was awarded an associate fellowship with the Higher Education Academy (AFHEA) in 2019 in acknowledgement of my commitment to teaching.
